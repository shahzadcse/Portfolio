VET Middle East continues to draw more visitors 12% increase for 2013

The 2013 edition of VET Middle East closed doors on 28th March after attracting 12% more visitors to the event as compared to last year. VETme was inaugurated by H.E AbdulRahman Mohammed Al Oweis, UAE Minister of Health at the Sheikh Saeed Halls of the Dubai International Exhibition Centre. The opening ceremony was graced by commercial counselors and trade advisors from a number of countries.

Exhibitor feedback for the event has been excellent with many reporting onsite orders for products and services on offer. The exhibitors this year have shown great satisfaction at the quality of visitors and the enquiries gathered during the event.
